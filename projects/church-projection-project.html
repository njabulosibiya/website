A little about the project...

The local church I go to is in no way a big church, with less than 150 church members. The church uses all the church equipment you'd expect from a small church, microphones for the singers, speakers speakers, keyboard etc, but one thing that we also have is a projection system. The church uses 2 Large TVs to display all the bible verses, the lyrics of the songs being sang and church announcements and the like. Its very helpful, especially for lyrics, since its a multi ethnic church with a variety of langueges spoken by the people, seeing the lyrics is convenient. I myself am responsible for displaying the aforementioned.
